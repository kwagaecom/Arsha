Admission Details

  • Nursery to Grade 5
  • Grade 6 to Grade 8
  • Grade 9 to Grade 12

Nursery to Grade 5

Our school provides a nurturing environment for young learners from Nursery to Grade 5. We focus on holistic development, encouraging creativity, and foundational learning. Admissions for these classes are open throughout the year, depending on the availability of seats.

  • Age Criteria: Nursery - 3 years+, Kindergarten - 4 years+
  • Documents Required: Birth Certificate, 2 Passport Size Photographs, Vaccination Record
  • Admission Process: Interaction with parents and child
  • Application Deadline: Rolling admissions, apply early

Grade 6 to Grade 8

The Middle School program emphasizes academic excellence and character building. We prepare students for the transition to higher grades by developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Age Criteria: Minimum 10 years
  • Documents Required: Birth Certificate, Previous School Records, Transfer Certificate
  • Admission Process: Written test in English and Mathematics, followed by an interview
  • Application Deadline: January 31st of each academic year

Grade 9 to Grade 12

Our High School program offers a rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for college and beyond. We offer a range of subjects and extra-curricular activities to ensure all-round development.

  • Age Criteria: Minimum 14 years
  • Documents Required: Previous School Records, Transfer Certificate, Identity Proof
  • Admission Process: Written test in core subjects, followed by a personal interview
  • Application Deadline: December 31st of each academic year

Note: Admissions are based on seat availability and meeting the eligibility criteria. Early application is recommended to secure a place. For further details, please contact our admission office or visit our website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school, admission process, curriculum, and more. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us.

  • How do I enroll my child at your school?

    You can enroll your child by visiting our admissions office or applying online through our website. Our admissions team will guide you through the process and provide all the necessary information.

  • What is the curriculum structure at your school?

    Our curriculum is based on a balanced blend of academic excellence, extracurricular activities, and personal development. We follow the national education standards and integrate modern teaching methods to enhance student learning.

  • How do you support students with special needs?

    We have a dedicated team of special educators who work closely with students with special needs. Our school provides individualized learning plans, counseling services, and appropriate resources to ensure every student receives the support they need.

  • What extracurricular activities are available?

    Our school offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, art, drama, and various clubs. These activities are designed to help students discover and develop their talents outside the classroom.

  • How do you ensure the safety of students?

    Student safety is our top priority. Our campus is equipped with surveillance cameras, and we have a strict security protocol. Additionally, we conduct regular safety drills and have a full-time nurse and trained staff to handle any emergencies.
